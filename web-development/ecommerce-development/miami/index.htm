    Best Practices From Our Miami eCommerce Experts

    Consider our in-house eCommerce best practices to build a successful online store.

    Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
    Customized Dashboards
    Data Control
    Security & Backups

    Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

    Follow SEO best practices by identifying target keywords and using them to optimize image tags, title tags, meta descriptions and headers. These simple steps can improve your website’s ranking on search engine results pages (SERPs) and in turn, increase visibility for your brand.

    Customized Dashboards

    Personalize your e-store admin dashboard to get the information you need quickly and at a glance, from pending orders and stock levels to revenue and more. This will allow you to make decisions and act upon them as quickly as possible, with the information you need at hand.

    Data Control

    Craft an eCommerce store that search engines can easily understand and position at the top of search engine results pages. One way to do this is to automate the process of creating title tags, which will help you add multiple product pages without wasting time.

    Security & Backups

    To guarantee that your company and your customers are protected at all times, follow the latest security policies and best practices. Run regular backup checks and install new security features as they arise, to help build trust with your customers.
